  • PC Games from the '90s, especially RPGs (e.g. Ultima 7, Baldur's Gate, Planescape Torment) and adventure games (e.g. Full Throttle, King's Quest, Grim Fandango)
  • Literary fiction (e.g. Haruki Murakami, Philip Roth), and some choice poetry (A Shropshire Lad, The Dream Songs)
  • British comedy (e.g. The Fast Show, Ideal, Garth Marengi's Darkplace)
  • Test match cricket, especially during the Australian summer
